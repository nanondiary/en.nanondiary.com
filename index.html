Biography

Nanon Korapat Kirdpan was born on 18 December 2000. He is the son of Thai actor Khunakorn Kirdpan He has one younger sister named Pitchaporn Kirdpan (Nonnie).

Nanon completed his secondary education at Amatyakul School. He is currently studying in acting and directing for cinema from the College of Social Communication Innovation at Srinakharinwirot University.

Nanon began his career at the age of three by appearing in several TV commercials and magazines. In 2015, Nanon signed a contract with GMMTV and had his first full role in Ugly Duckling: Don't as Plawan, the brother of the female lead.

Afterward, Nanon played several supporting roles, Wonder Teacher, Love Flight. He later become known for leading roles in Secret Love: My Lil Boy & My Lil Boy 2 as S, My Dear Loser: Edge of 17 as Oh, The Gifted as Pang, and Blacklist as Traffic.
